The idea behind Scratch is that kids could experience the joy of building their own project "from scratch" and watch it come to life.

I used it for a variety of things, but as an example, here is a small game I made in 2014

Controls: Arrow keys to move and jump, Space to switch sides, Toggle music with "m", Toggle blur with "b", Restart level with "r"
